Umbilical

This game is in constant change, and will definitely change more until the final release!
You may wishlist to check the end result!

Steam Page

This is a 3rd person game about protecting the connection that joins the playable character and the game camera, also personified as a character.

As I worked on it, my responsibilities were the following:

  • Take part in the first public playtest of the existing build, gather data and confront it with the director’s vision.
  • Non-Digital Prototypes of gameplay, searching for fun mechanics and puzzles to do with the Camera-Character-Umbilical Cord triad
  • Rulesets and guidelines of the proposed mechanics
  • Whitebox of a ‘playground area’, where we could playtest it and check different use cases and expand upon them
  • “Horror triggers”: small events that we could put in the game and scare the player and test it - lights, sounds, etc
