Viking Days

Minigame bundle released originally to Gear VR and then massively updated to Quest 2.

Steam Link

This project's fascinating pipeline stimulates and inserts the collective intelligence of every dev into it, as they all come with concepts for minigames, and those are refined through design and playtest.

My responsabilities were the following:
  • Guiding the collective intelligence into the design pillars of the game, conducting brainstorms for example;
  • Turning concepts into actionable, reachable verbs, rulesets, and parameters that may potentially emerge gameplay
  • Conducting playtest and feedback loop;
  • Guidelines for game feel and easy affordance;
  • Balancing;
